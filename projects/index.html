Projects

In May of 2018, I wanted to put together a small portable speaker, incorporating Bluetooth functionality while maintaining a more natural wood aesthetic than that of many portable speakers.

Luckily, Parts Express carries a compact combination Bluetooth-amplifier board. The board, along with a Li-Ion battery pack, three LEDs, a rotational potentiometer, a DC jack, a 3.5mm audio jack, a SPST toggle switch, and two matching speaker drivers rounded out the electrical components.

On the woodworking side of the project, I bought some appearance plywood from Home Depot, cut the pieces to the sizes I needed, and routed the holes for the back panel and the speaker drivers. I then stained the wood, and added a cheap cabinet handle as an easy way to carry the speaker.

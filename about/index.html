About Me

Bio

Hey! I'm Miles Harber, a senior at the University of Michigan (Go Blue!). I'm studying Mechanical Engineering, and hope to pursue that after graduation. Currently, I'm a Reliability Engineering Intern performing Fault Tree Analysis with the Product and Circuit Reliability Analysis Group (5139) at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL).





Interests

Within engineering, I'm particularly interested in dynamics and controls, as well as mechatronic system design and analysis. Outside of the classroom (or machine shop), I'm a captain on the Michigan Men's Club Water Polo team, and I am a mechanical designer with Michigan Neuroprosthetics.
